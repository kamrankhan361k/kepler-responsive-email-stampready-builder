StampReady

1. After you log-in your account. At the " CAMPAIGNS " click at " New Campaign ". [1]

2. Then click " UPLOAD TEMPLATE " button and select " ZIP FILE ". [2]

3. Select a Zip file in the StampReady folder.

4. Then click " USE TEMPLATE " button. [3]

5. Name' s your campaign. [4]

6. Then click " START EDITING " button. [5]

7. Check the template and edit, and click " SAVE " button to done. [6]

Export To MailChimp

StampReady export to MailChimp

1. After you upload your template and customize style, choose section. Click at "To Desktop".  [1]

2. Name' s your template. [2]

3. Then click " KEEP IMAGES ONLINE" (Recommend) or "INCLUDE IN FOLDER" button. [3]

4. After you download file complete. 
 
5. Login to MailChimp
 
6. Click Create Campaign. Then fill your information.
 
7. Then click "Code Your Own" [4]
 
 
8. Click select button at "Import from zip" [5]
 
9. Choose file from StampReady Builder (Exported file). Upload it!  [6]

ActiveCampaign

1. After you log-in your account, click at " Campaigns " on tab menu. [1]

2. Then select " Manage Template ". [2]

3. Then click at " New Template ". [3]

4. Name your template and select " Custom HTML ". [4]

5. Then copy and paste code on the left section. You can see preview on the right section. Template setting and revision preview on the top right icon. Save and Exit done! 

All images must hosting. Change all the image link to absolute URL (http://www.yourimagehost.com/…/…) not rerative URL (images/logo.jpg)

FAQ

Why background images are not show in Outlook?

Outlook is not support background-image since version 2007 acording to this Email CSS Support Guide. It will replace with a solid background color.

Is it possible to change background images in Mailchimp?

MailChimp don't have Change Background function. If you want change background you can use StampReady Builder export to MailChimp.

I've been trying to use google webfonts in MailChimp but I get this error when trying to preview.

Example :

It turns out it's not possible through the @import syntax. It does work using tag:

<link href='https://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Montserrat:400,700' rel='stylesheet' type='text/css'>
<link href='https://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Open+Sans:400,600' rel='stylesheet' type='text/css'>

1. After you export your template from StampReady. Open index.html [Export from StampReady] in Text Editor.

2.  Delete Highlight Area.

3. Add this code (Below here) before <style>

<link href='https://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Montserrat:400,700' rel='stylesheet' type='text/css'>
<link href='https://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Open+Sans:400,600' rel='stylesheet' type='text/css'>

4. Then Upload to MailChimp.
